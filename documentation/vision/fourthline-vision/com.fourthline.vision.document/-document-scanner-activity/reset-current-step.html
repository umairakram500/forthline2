resetCurrentStep

Content copied to clipboard

Resets current step. It will trigger DocumentScannerCallback.onStepUpdate It requires that current step is finished, means either of DocumentScannerCallback.onSuccess or DocumentScannerCallback.onFail were called.

If used incorrectly, DocumentScanner will fail with RESET_CURRENT_STEP_NOT_ALLOWED. See DocumentScannerStepError
