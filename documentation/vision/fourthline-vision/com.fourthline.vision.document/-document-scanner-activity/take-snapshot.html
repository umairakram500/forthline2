Content copied to clipboard

Takes snapshot manually. It will trigger DocumentScannerCallback.onStepSuccess. It requires that current step is active, means neither of DocumentScannerCallback.onStepSuccess or DocumentScannerCallback.onStepFail were called after DocumentScannerCallback.onStepUpdate

If used incorrectly, DocumentScanner will fail with TAKE_SNAPSHOT_NOT_ALLOWED. See DocumentScannerStepError
